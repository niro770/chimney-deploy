What We Do

Chimney damage left unrepaired leads to water intrusion, structural deterioration and fire hazards. Most repairs are significantly less expensive when caught early — which is why annual inspection is the best investment you can make in chimney longevity. We repair all masonry chimney systems and factory-built units.

  • Crown repair & replacement: Cracked or deteriorated crowns sealed or rebuilt
  • Flashing repair: Failed flashing re-sealed or replaced to stop water entry
  • Firebox repair: Cracked firebricks and mortar joints restored
  • Spalling brick: Deteriorating brick faces stabilized and replaced
  • Smoke chamber parging: Smooth, code-compliant smoke chamber restoration
  • Damper repair: Broken or frozen dampers repaired or replaced
