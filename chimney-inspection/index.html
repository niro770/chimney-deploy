What We Do

A chimney inspection is required before using a fireplace after any home purchase, major storm, chimney fire, or extended period of non-use. NFPA 211 defines three levels of inspection based on the extent of examination required.

  • Level 1: Visual inspection of accessible portions
  • Level 2: Interior scanning and video inspection
  • Level 3: Invasive inspection for serious damage
  • Assessment of chimney structure and lining
  • Firebox, smoke chamber and damper check
  • Written inspection report with photos
