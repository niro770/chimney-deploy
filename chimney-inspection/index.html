What We Do

NFPA 211 defines three levels of chimney inspection. Level 1 covers accessible portions and is standard with every cleaning. Level 2 — required when selling a home or after any chimney event — includes video camera scanning of the full flue interior. Level 3 is invasive and reserved for serious structural concerns. Every inspection includes a written report with photos.

  • Level 1 — Visual: Accessible areas, firebox, damper, smoke chamber
  • Level 2 — Video: Full camera scan of flue interior, required for home sales
  • Level 3 — Invasive: For serious structural damage investigation
  • Written report: Findings documented with photos after every inspection
  • Same-day available: Most markets have same-day inspection appointments
