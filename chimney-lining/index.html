What We Do

A properly functioning chimney liner is critical for safe operation. Liners contain combustion gases, protect the masonry from heat and corrosion, and provide the right draft for your appliance. Cracked, deteriorated or missing liners must be repaired or replaced.

  • Stainless steel flexible liner installation
  • Rigid stainless liner for straight chimneys
  • HeatShield ceramic liner restoration
  • Liner sizing for your appliance
  • Insulation wrap for improved draft
  • Full liner replacement for deteriorated clay tile
