What We Do

A functional chimney liner is critical for safe operation. Liners contain combustion gases, protect masonry from heat and corrosion, and provide correct draft for your appliance. Cracked, deteriorated or missing liners must be replaced. Many older homes have clay tile liners now 80–130 years old that have never been replaced — only a camera inspection reveals their true condition.

  • Flexible stainless liner: For chimneys with offsets — most common installation
  • Rigid stainless liner: For straight chimneys — maximum durability
  • HeatShield restoration: Ceramic liner repair — alternative to full relining
  • Insulation wrap: Improves draft and efficiency for all liner types
  • Liner sizing: BTU and flue diameter matched to your specific appliance
  • Full installation: Complete from appliance connection to cap — one visit
