A Full-Service Chimney Company, Wherever You Are

Your chimney does a serious job. It carries heat, smoke and combustion gases safely out of your home, and when any part of that system fails, the result can be a house fire, a carbon monoxide risk, or water damage that spreads through your walls. That is why we built ChimneyServices.net as a single, certified team that handles the whole system, from a routine sweep to a full masonry rebuild, in more than 40 cities across the country.

Every technician we send is certified by the Chimney Safety Institute of America (CSIA), the industry's recognized credential. That means they are trained specifically in chimney and venting systems and tested on the standards that keep your fireplace safe, including NFPA 211, the National Fire Protection Association code for chimneys, fireplaces and venting. We are also licensed, bonded and insured in every market we serve.

Why Annual Chimney Care Matters

The National Fire Protection Association recommends that every chimney be inspected at least once a year and cleaned whenever creosote buildup reaches about one-eighth of an inch. The reason is simple. Burning wood leaves behind creosote, a flammable residue that coats the inside of the flue, and it is the leading cause of chimney fires. An annual visit removes that buildup and catches small problems, like a hairline crack in the crown or a worn flashing seal, while they are still inexpensive to fix. Skip it for a few seasons and those small problems become the expensive ones.
