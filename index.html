Choosing the Right Chimney Service

A few things to consider when caring for your chimney:

  • Annual Inspection: Chimneys should be inspected at least once a year to catch cracks, blockages, and creosote before they become hazards.
  • Creosote Levels: Heavy creosote buildup is highly flammable — regular sweeping dramatically reduces fire risk.
  • Liner Condition: A damaged flue liner can leak dangerous gases. Stainless or cast-in-place liners restore safe venting.
  • Cap & Crown: A quality cap keeps out rain, debris, and animals; a sound crown prevents water damage to the masonry.
  • Waterproofing: Sealing brick and mortar protects against freeze-thaw cracking and costly structural repairs.
  • Certified Technicians: Always choose CSIA-certified, insured professionals for inspections and repairs.
