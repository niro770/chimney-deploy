Frequently Asked Questions

Common chimney questions answered by our certified technicians.

The NFPA recommends chimneys be inspected and cleaned at least once per year, regardless of how often the fireplace is used. Heavy wood burners should clean every 50–70 fires.

Chimney cleaning typically costs $100–$300 depending on your chimney type, location and level of buildup. A standard Level 1 inspection is included. Call (855) 807-7707 for a free local estimate.

Yes — same-day chimney cleaning, inspection and repair is available in most of our 40+ markets. Call (855) 807-7707 and we will do our best to schedule a technician the same day.

CSIA (Chimney Safety Institute of America) certification is the industry's highest credential. Our technicians pass rigorous written exams and hands-on assessments, with continuing education required to maintain certification.

Annual inspection is recommended for all chimneys. A Level 2 inspection is required when selling a home, when changing fuel types, or after any chimney fire or significant storm event.
