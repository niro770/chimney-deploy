Frequently Asked Questions

Everything homeowners want to know about chimney service.

The NFPA recommends chimneys be inspected at least once a year and cleaned as needed. For homes using a fireplace regularly, annual cleaning is standard. Wood-burning fireplaces used frequently may need cleaning more often.

Chimney cleaning typically costs between $100 and $300 depending on the type of fireplace, level of buildup, and your location. A standard Level 1 inspection and sweeping averages around $150–$200 for most homes.

A standard chimney cleaning and inspection takes about 45 minutes to 1.5 hours for most homes. More extensive cleanings or repairs may take longer.

Signs your chimney needs cleaning include: a strong smoky smell even when not in use, poor draft causing smoke to enter the room, black soot visible in the firebox, reduced fireplace efficiency, and animal noises or debris in the flue.

Yes — we offer same-day chimney cleaning, inspection, and repair services in most service areas. Call (855) 807-7707 and we will do our best to schedule a technician the same day.

Our certified technicians use professional drop cloths, high-powered HEPA vacuums, and containment equipment to keep your home clean. When done correctly, chimney cleaning should leave no mess in your home.
